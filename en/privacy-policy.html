Relicer Privacy Policy



Privacy Policy

  • This service collects and records game IDs by associating them with Discord user IDs to obtain in-game information.
  • If you wish to update the recorded data, please enter a new valid value in the input field for the respective data. The old data will be automatically overwritten and deleted.
  • If you wish to delete the recorded data, please notify us posting in the official server or sending DM to dev team member. We will delete the desired data from our storage.
  • The collected UID and in-game data will not be provided to third parties, except when disclosure is required by law.
  • Images generated through the use of this service may be used for advertising by the dev team after being anonymized.
  • If you have any questions, please feel free to contact dev team.
