Introduction of Relicer

What is Relicer?


Relicer is utility bot for Genshin Impact / Honkai: Star Rail and Zenless Zone Zero.
You can rate your relic from UID, check your in-game profile from discord, calculate energy recovery time, etc.
Relic scoring is for heavy players.

ZZZ buildcard is temporate, which requires you to input all data by yourself. Fetching feature from UID will be available when API releases from provider.
